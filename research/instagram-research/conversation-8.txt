Nguyen (Selected Answer):
I also created a bug ticket for Facebook and after a long discussion they said that although they support files less than 1GB to upload, but we can't guarantee the upload will finish successfully, so an alternative way is to limit the size to 200MB

Please see more detail in https://developers.facebook.com/support/bugs/489447820268760/
