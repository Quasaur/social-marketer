Title: Reels videos are stuck in "IN_PROGRESS" status for hours

=== CONTENT ===

Grant (Original Poster):
We are seeing an increase in users reporting that their Reels videos are timing out. We upload them to Instagram, then the videos get stuck in the "IN_PROGRESS" status for 3 hours, after which we mark them as "timed out."

For the users that are affected by this bug, this issue happens to all of their Reels.

Here are the steps we take:

We upload media to Instagram to get back a container ID.
We check the container status and get back a status_code of IN_PROGRESS.
We continue to check every 5 minutes for 3 hours, and the video never leaves the IN_PROGRESS status.

This makes Postpone unusable for users who want to publish Reels and are affected by this bug. These users report having no restrictions on their account under Account Status in Instagram.
