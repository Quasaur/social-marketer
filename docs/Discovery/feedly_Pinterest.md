Using Feedly & Pinterest

Video: <https://youtu.be/62f0W5yrpP4?si=-Leul4cdyu5PjihF>

Summary

This video presents a content marketing workflow (0:00) that leverages Feedly and Pinterest to drive traffic to a website.

The presenter outlines the following steps:
• Following personal blogs in Feedly (0:14): This allows for easy tracking and processing of one's own content.
• Utilizing Feedly's Pinterest integration (0:24): This feature enables simultaneous posting of content to Pinterest, Facebook, and Twitter by simply clicking "pin it" (0:32). Feedly automatically pulls the post title and image (0:39).
• Amplifying content and driving traffic (0:47): By using Feedly and Pinterest, the content is amplified, drawing people back to the original website.
• Double-dipping on visual content (2:02): The process involves re-expressing or amplifying already used or posted visual content to maximize its reach.
• Tracing the traffic flow (2:48): The presenter demonstrates how content pinned from Feedly appears on Pinterest, Facebook, and Twitter (3:28). Clicking these posts leads users to the Pinterest pin (3:42), and then to the original website (3:56).

The core message is that by strategically sharing content on social media, using the website as a central hub, all clicks can ultimately lead back to the site, encouraging further engagement.
